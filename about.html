What is this?

A walkable path from San Francisco to New York City is about 2918 miles, and I'm going to walk that distance on my treadmill on stream. I am a Tread Man Walking.

Who are you?

I'm Shawn, also known as RitzPlays on the Internet. I'm 21 and live in San Francisco. I am a streamer and Youtuber who plans to walk 2918 miles while streaming myself playing games on Twitch. I'll be playing PC, Xbox, PlayStation, and Wii games.

Why are you doing this?

Each month I am walking, I will be raising money for a different charity. To start things off for January we will be benefiting Child's Play! Click the donate button to donate to them!

What can I do?

Donate if you can. Click on the map tab to view the map and see the path. If I'm walking through a city/town near you, click the "Contact" button and email me so I can ask you a few questions about the area and possibly interview you on stream!
